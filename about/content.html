Mark Emil Poulsen is a graphic designer currently based in Copenhagen. His work is content driven; motivated by approaching graphic design as a form of close reading that engages in a visual, editorial and conceptual dialogue with somebody else’s work. He holds a BA in Art & Design from the Gerrit Rietveld Academie in Amsterdam.
