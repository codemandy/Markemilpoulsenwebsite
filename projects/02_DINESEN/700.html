Nothing Comes From Nothing: Objects, Stories, and Encounters from the Dinesen Archive. Concept, graphic design, editing (in conversation with Hans Peter Dinesen and Jens Jacob Dinesen), printing and binding (in collaboration with Actum Kopi & Tryk and Benjamin Savi). Texts by Peter Møller Rasmussen. Published by Dinesen Legacy. 2025 Shortlisted for “Best Book Design from all over the World 2025” by Stiftung Buchkunst (Leipzig). Selected for “Årets bedste bogarbejde / Best Danish Book Design 2025” by Forening for boghaandværk (Copenhagen)
